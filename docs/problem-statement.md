# Problem Statement

## Overview

Technology has become an essential part of everyday life, yet many people still struggle with using smartphones confidently. Whether it's making a video call, connecting to Wi-Fi, installing an app, or adjusting simple settings, these everyday tasks can feel overwhelming for someone who didn't grow up with digital technology or has limited experience using it.

StayWithMe is a project born from a simple idea: learning technology shouldn't feel lonely or intimidating. Instead of expecting people to figure things out through trial and error, StayWithMe aims to provide calm, step-by-step guidance that helps users build confidence while learning at their own pace.

## The Problem

Smartphones have become gateways to communication, healthcare, banking, education, and countless everyday services. Despite this, many people are left behind because most digital experiences assume users already understand how smartphones work.

Instructions are often filled with unfamiliar terms, support articles can be difficult to follow, and video tutorials usually require users to pause, rewind, and replay multiple times. For someone learning for the first time, even simple tasks like adjusting settings, connecting to Wi-Fi, or installing an application can become frustrating.

As a result, many people rely heavily on family members or friends for basic digital tasks. Others avoid using technology altogether because they fear making mistakes or simply don't know where to begin.

## Who Is Affected?

StayWithMe is designed for anyone who needs a little more patience and guidance while learning technology, including:

- Older adults becoming more active in the digital world.
- People transitioning from feature phones to smartphones.
- Individuals with limited digital literacy.
- First-time smartphone users.
- Anyone who feels anxious or overwhelmed when learning new technology.

Although these groups have different experiences, they all share one common need: learning without fear of judgment.

## Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

There are already many resources available to help people learn technology, but most are designed for users who already have a basic understanding of smartphones.

Many tutorials move too quickly, use technical language without explanation, or assume users already know where to find settings and recognize icons. Written guides often feel overwhelming, while video tutorials require constant pausing and replaying.

When these resources fall short, many people have no choice but to repeatedly ask family or friends for help. While this support is valuable, it isn't always available when it's needed.

Learning technology should feel supportive, patient, and encouraging—not stressful or intimidating.

## Our Vision

> *"What if technology could patiently walk alongside people instead of expecting them to already know what to do?"*

That question is the foundation of StayWithMe.

Our vision is to create a learning experience that feels less like reading a manual and more like having a trusted companion beside you—someone who explains each step clearly, encourages you when you're unsure, and helps you build confidence one task at a time.

## Project Goal

The goal of StayWithMe is to research, design, and build an accessible digital companion that helps people learn everyday smartphone tasks through clear, supportive, and easy-to-follow guidance.

Rather than simply providing answers, StayWithMe aims to help users understand what they are doing, why they are doing it, and gradually become more independent when using technology.

## Success Looks Like

Success for StayWithMe goes beyond helping someone complete a task.

Success means helping people become more confident using technology on their own.

We will consider this project successful when users begin to:

- Complete everyday smartphone tasks independently.
- Feel less anxious when exploring new features.
- Build confidence instead of relying entirely on others.
- Stay connected with family, friends, and essential digital services.

Ultimately, StayWithMe hopes to make technology feel more approachable, more human, and more empowering—one step at a time.
