# User Personas

## Purpose

User personas help keep StayWithMe focused on the people it is being built for. While these personas are fictional, they represent real situations and challenges experienced by many individuals learning to use smartphones.

# Persona 1: Mary

**Age:** 72

**Background:**

Mary recently received her first smartphone from her children so they could stay connected through video calls and messaging apps. Although excited, she often feels overwhelmed by unfamiliar icons and settings.

**Goals**

- Learn how to make video calls independently.
- Send and receive photos from family.
- Use messaging apps with confidence.

**Challenges**

- Afraid of tapping the wrong button.
- Finds technology terminology confusing.
- Often forgets the sequence of steps after someone explains them.

# Persona 2: Joseph

**Age:** 64

**Background:**

Joseph recently retired and wants to become more comfortable using digital services such as online banking, appointment booking, and government services.

**Goals**

- Complete everyday digital tasks independently.
- Build confidence using smartphone features.
- Reduce dependence on family members for technical help.

**Challenges**

- Doesn't know where to start.
- Feels embarrassed asking for repeated help.
- Gets frustrated when instructions move too quickly.

# Persona 3: Amina

**Age:** 48

**Background:**

Amina has recently transitioned from a feature phone to a smartphone. She wants to make better use of digital tools for work and communication but finds the learning process intimidating.

**Goals**

- Learn smartphone basics.
- Understand common applications.
- Feel confident exploring new features.

**Challenges**

- Limited digital experience.
- Unsure what different icons mean.
- Worries about making irreversible mistakes.

## What These Personas Teach Us

Although each person has different experiences, they all share similar needs:

- Clear and simple guidance.
- Patient, step-by-step learning.
- Encouragement rather than criticism.
- Confidence through practice.
- An experience that respects their pace of learning.

These shared needs guide every design and development decision made throughout StayWithMe.
